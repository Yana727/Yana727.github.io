Why I became a developer

My first choice of a career was teaching. I loved math and working with children, so it was a natural choice for me as a young adult. However teaching Middle School Math for the last seven years, I was grew disappointed with the public school system. I loved teaching and inspiring my students, but could not stand the “test-driven” environment and focus on standardized scores, rather than the students themselves. So I decided to switch careers. I wasn’t sure what I wanted to do next and therefore did my research. Coding seem to combine my two passions: the love of problem solving and technology (which I believe is the future).
